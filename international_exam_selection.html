Choose an International Exam

USMLE (United States Medical Licensing Examination)

The USMLE is a three-step examination for medical licensure in the United States.

MRCP (Membership of the Royal Colleges of Physicians)

A postgraduate medical diploma in the United Kingdom and many other countries.

PLAB (Professional and Linguistic Assessments Board)

An exam to demonstrate that international doctors have the necessary skills and knowledge to practice medicine in the UK.

FMGE (Foreign Medical Graduate Examination)

A screening test for Indian citizens who have obtained their primary medical qualification from outside India.
